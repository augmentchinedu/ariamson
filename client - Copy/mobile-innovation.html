MOBILE
AND INNOVATION

We are involved in original design, ODM manufacture, assembling of “ZONIC” brand Mobile Phones in collaboration with our Partners (IMG Technology Group); an internationally recognized mobile phone developer and ODM manufacturer with core competitiveness in OEM, ODM, OBM, and certified with SGS ISO9001: 2008 Quality Management Systems. We also handle design and manufacture of mobile phone devices for mobile phone operators and other clients.

Our Group is focussed on innovation and high tech solutions to problems facing mobile phone users in sub-Sahara African market, to address the needs of the market and phone users, and provide more efficient service delivery and customer care. “ZONIC” mobile phones are solution-driven, locally focused with global quality and European Union (EU) certification.

The official launch of “ZONIC” Flagship Mobile Smart and Feature Phones is being planned for the second quarter of 2021. More information about “ZONIC” Mobile Phones can be found on www.zonicimgmobile.com

Ariamson in collaboration with its partners provide free video and voice chat application on Android, IOS and Windows, and Tablets with innovative features including international voice and video calls, videoconference, and photo sharing contact album for users.
