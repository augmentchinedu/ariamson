INFRASTRUCTURE

Our Infrastructure services relate to social and economic infrastructure and energy
related projects.

Energy & Power

We have interest in power generation, transmission, distribution, and alternative energy. We are professionals and very competitive with added capacities from our strategic alliance and collaboration with our seasoned partners.

In Ariamson, we have the capacity to analyse power generation, transmission and distribution infrastructures and offer appropriate advice on establishing plant and solutions that will resolve any present and future power issues. We are also able to carry out various studies depending on need including: study of current loads and generation capacity in a country or region, network capacity, reliability and security, options for increasing capacity of exiting lines, connecting distributed generation – long lines, control, protection, fault levels, and stability. In addition, we undertake feasibilities studies in respect of power provision (both on grid and off grid) and provide bankable feasibility studies for funding purposes. We are currently in talks with some of our European and Canadian Partners on alternative energy sources which are not only affordable, sustainable but environmentally friendly.

Services:

  • Consulting
  • Power generation and distribution

In collaboration with our Partners, we supply and market indoor and outdoor electrical products, including vacuum circuit breakers, aluminium conductors, switchgear panels, and power transformers.

Real Estate

Ariamson’s properties vision is to be in the forefront of real estate development in Nigeria, providing high quality, most comfortable and affordable residential and commercial properties across the country. In particular, we are involved in the building of high quality and affordable housing for high, medium and low income earners.

In We are focused on property development and management, marketing and sales of real estate products, and facility management.
