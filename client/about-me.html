Dr. Anthony Maduka (PhD, FCMI) Executive Chairman & CEO

Dr. Maduka is a pioneer Director and Founder of Ariamson Group. He is an innovative, skilled strategist, result-oriented, ambitious, and conscientious Entrepreneur and Senior Executive Manager with 28 years’ management and leadership experience in public and private sectors, including 12 years’ at the strategic and Board-level from an international perspective.

He graduated Bachelor of Science (BSc) Hons degree in Business Administration (Second Class Upper) from the University of Benin Nigeria, Master of Science (MSc) degree in Management, and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Management both from London South Bank University, United Kingdom. Anthony is also a Fellow of Chartered Management Institute and has undergone one year of a two-year Master at Laws (Qualifying Law Degree) programme at the University of London. He has attended many management and leadership programmes, and presented many management papers at many conferences across Europe, USA and Africa.

Anthony has held several management and leadership positions including Deputy Head of Scrutiny and Overview at London Government, Principal Consultant at Prima Consult, and President & CEO at Beaton Group all in the UK. Also, he is growing a multi-sectoral international conglomerate; operating a chain of twelve companies which he founded. Anthony has led and participated in serval multi-million dollar projects in telecoms and mobile, power, minerals, commodities and infrastructure across the world.

Dr. Maduka is financially astute, with a proven ability to set and monitor large budgets and analyse financial data to inform policy development and decision making. He negotiates effectively at all levels, building robust relationships with colleagues, stakeholders, and government authorities, and has developed and implemented effective business strategies that delivered growth, improvement and cost efficiency.

He is recognized as a decisive and motivational leader who is able to recruit, develop and retain cohesive teams focused on achieving organisational objectives. Confident managing high profile change projects, aligning the workforce with key business strategies and implementing coherent systems and processes. He combines international experience with natural aptitude for innovation and solutions for improved services and organisation success.

Anthony brings his 12 years’ Board-level experience, his extensive commercial acumen and operational experience, combined with senior leadership skills and proven ability to deliver results and change in the affairs of the Board
