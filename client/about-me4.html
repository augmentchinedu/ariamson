Brig Gen Chris Ilouga (Rtd) MSc, FCM Director of Strategy

Brig-General Chris (Retired) had an accomplished career in the Nigerian Army; rising from the rank of Second Lieutenant to Brigadier General in his 31 years of meritorious military service . He is a graduate of Nigerian Defence Academy, National Defence College, and holds Master of Science (MSc) degree in Strategic Studies from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria. He is decorated with Field Command Medal (FCM), and a Universal Peace Federation Award of Peace Ambassador.

Chris attended all mandatory courses with respect to Regular Combatant Commission in the Nigerian Army including the National Defence Course, and held some key Command and Staff Appointments within and outside Nigeria. These include Adjutant 321 Artillery Regiment, ECOMOG in Liberia, Commanding Officer 331 Artillery Regiment, Team Leader United Nations Mission in Sudan, Assistant Director of Training (land) Defence Headquarters, Commander 31 Artillery Brigade, and Deputy Director of Research & Development at Nigerian Defence Headquarters.

In retirement, Chris carried his experience into the private sector and business. He sits on the board of many companies including SNOPSIS Technologies Ltd, SKAFNET Concepts Ltd, and TAKAOB Technologies Ltd. He is a distinguished Tactician and brings his experience in leadership, strategy and operations, amongst others, to our Team and Organization.
