Marcel Nwonu MSc, MBA, LLM, ACA Director (Finance & Admin)

Marcel has a multi-disciplinary capacity in economics, business administration, finance, accounting, law, audit and consultancy, and is a great asset to the Team. He graduated with Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Economics, specializing in Econometrics and Quantitative Analysis, and Master of Science (MSc) degree in Economics, with specialization in Project Analysis and Evaluation, both from the University of Lagos, Lagos-Nigeria. He took up professional accounting and since 1992, has been a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He is also a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales (ACA)..

Marcel later proceeded to the United Kingdom where he obtained a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from London South Bank University, and Master at Laws (LLM) from University of London. He is currently a PhD candidate in Critical Management at Lancaster University, Lancaster in the United Kingdom, researching on “Learning and Management Processes in Organizations”.

Marcel has an outstanding experience in audit and consulting services having worked for Akintola Williams & Co, a firm of chartered accountants in Nigeria, UAC Plc Lagos Nigeria, Reunion Group, Lagos Nigeria, Anderson Consulting London, UK, and Prima Consultants in the UK where he worked as a Consulting Partner. He was Senior Consultant to Uthman Kay, a firm of Chartered Certified Accountants in England and Wales.

Marcel’s vast experience includes business health assessment and project management, personnel and financial need assessment, audit and risk management, strategic and business planning, mergers, acquisition, consolidation, business/project evaluation, and sensitivity analysis, to mention but a few. He brings his vast experience and capacity to bear on our Team.
