Sydney AhamefulaMSc, PGD, FAAT, CFADirector (Operations & Customer Services

Sydney holds Master of Science (MSc) degree in Strategic Financial Management from Kingston University, and Post-Graduate Diploma in Management Studies from London South Bank University, both in the United Kingdom. Also, he has been a Certified Fraud Examiner in the United Kingdom and a Fellow of the Association of Accounting Technicians

Sydney is a proactive, results-driven management professional with transferable skills who has led end to end projects and programmes, overcome obstacles to successfully manage change and strategic direction; motivated staff, empowered community leaders, developed and implemented strategies and increased financial efficiency of organizations

He develops and advises on organizational anti-fraud strategic programmes, corporate risk management initiatives and business continuity programmes. Also, he is a specialist on best value programmes to ensure economy, efficiency and effectiveness.

Sydney brings to the Team his 23 years’ experience in business development, fraud prevention, financial management, planning and investigation, project management, human resources, team and capacity building, stakeholders’ management, regeneration, community cohesion, and diversity management. He is also a motivation speaker and workshop specialist.
